How to Play

🃏

Draw a Card

Tap to flip — one card per round, no peeking ahead.

🎭

Card Types

Challenge — Do it or drink. Truth — Answer or drink twice. Vote — Both vote secretly. Couple — Do it together.

🍹

Drinking Rules

1 sip = 1 drink. Failing a challenge? 2 sips. Refusing a truth? 3 sips. No judges here.

🏆

Rounds

10 rounds per game. After round 5 — the Spicy Zone kicks in. Cards get bolder.
