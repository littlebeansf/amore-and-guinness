How to Play

🃏

Draw a Card

One card per round — no peeking ahead. In Player Turns mode, players alternate. In Shared Rounds, every card involves both of you.

🎭

Card Types

Challenge — Do it or drink. Truth — Answer or drink twice. Vote — Both vote secretly. Couple — Do it together. Wild — Chaos ensues.

🌡️

Three Zones

Warm-Up — light, funny, get-to-know-you vibes. Spicy Zone — things get personal. Extreme — no filter, no mercy.

🍹

Sip Rules

1 sip = 1 drink. Chicken out of a challenge: +2 sips. Refuse a truth: +3 sips. Open-ended cards let you adjust sips manually. No judges here.
